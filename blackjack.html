How to Play Blackjack

In Blackjack, your goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over. You'll compete against the dealer, and whoever is closest to 21 wins!

  • Ace can count as 1 or 11
  • Face cards (King, Queen, Jack) count as 10
  • Hit to receive another card or stand to stay with your hand
